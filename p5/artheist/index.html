Oasis

Lonesome cowboy just trying to save all the cats and avoid cacti

A Generative RPG experience

The player is a vigilantic cowboy that roams the desert. His quest is to save a cat thats trapped in the desert. The cowboy will have to save the cat and as a reward they get an art piece from the cat. Initially, the player cannot walk to the NPC because there are cacti that will slow the player down. As a result- they must change the map by drinking some water. The music will generate a unique sounding noise by calling a new seed each time. The music depends where the cactus will be placed- representing a midi player that is stretched out. All graphics are hand painted using watercolor.

Objective:
-Save the cat
-Avoid cacti so you don't go slower
-Drink some water to make some music(anyways, here's wonderwall)

Controls:
Movement - WASD keys or Arrow Keys
Yes - "Y" key
No - "N" key
